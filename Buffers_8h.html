Tools for allocating both temporary and permanent buffers.

Modern renderer has two buffer types:

  • Temporary (per frame) buffers
  • Permanent buffers Temporary buffers are only valid until the end of the frame (ie, you call mvr_PresentFrame), they are for things like temporary shapes or uniform buffers or things like that. Permanent buffers need to be manually managed by the user.
